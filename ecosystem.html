The Verified Carbon Standard (VCS) is the world’s leading voluntary carbon market program. It provides a framework for measuring, reporting, and verifying greenhouse gas (GHG) emission reductions and removals. Projects certified under the VCS generate Verified Carbon Units (VCUs), which can be traded and used by companies and organizations to offset their emissions.
Climate Impact X (CIX) is a global carbon credit trading platform, founded in Singapore by a joint venture between DBS Bank, Singapore Exchange (SGX), Standard Chartered and Temasek. Its objective is to expand the voluntary carbon market and promote nature-based climate solutions.
B3 (Brasil, Bolsa, Balcão) is the main stock, commodities, and futures exchange in Brazil, recognized as one of the largest financial market infrastructures in the world. Resulting from the merger between BM&FBovespa and CETIP in 2017, B3 provides a highly regulated and secure environment for trading financial assets, derivatives, and offering registration, custody, and settlement solutions.
Earthood is a global Validation and Verification Body (VVB) specializing in carbon offset verification, ESG consulting, and sustainable agriculture audits. Founded in 2012, it is accredited by major standards like Verra, Gold Standard, and UNFCCC. Earthood was named Best Verification Company by Environmental Finance in 2024 and 2025. The company operates in over 130 countries, with a strong focus on digital innovation and climate resilience.
Bureau Veritas is a global leader in testing, inspection, and certification (TIC) services. Founded in 1828 and headquartered in France, the company helps businesses ensure quality, health and safety, environmental protection, and social responsibility across a wide range of industries. Bureau Veritas operates in over 140 countries and is recognized for its role in verifying compliance with international standards, including carbon footprint verification and sustainability reporting.
EPTV (Emissoras Pioneiras de Televisão) is a Brazilian regional television network affiliated with TV Globo. It operates in the states of São Paulo and Minas Gerais, broadcasting news, entertainment, and cultural programming. Founded in 1979, EPTV is known for producing local content tailored to its regional audiences while maintaining the high standards of Globo's national network.
The Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) is an international financial center and free zone located on Al Maryah Island, in Abu Dhabi, capital of the United Arab Emirates. Established in 2013 and operational since October 2015, ADGM was created to promote Abu Dhabi's economic development and consolidate its position as a global financial hub.
Proph3t Capital is a venture capital firm focused on blockchain and Web3 technologies, especially aimed at the Latin American market.
Martinelli & Guimarães – Advocacia Contemporânea is a Curitiba‑based boutique law firm specializing in corporate and regulatory matters for both individuals and businesses. They offer services in civil litigation, government relations, digital law, data protection, contractual work, mergers & acquisitions, licensing, and public procurement.
Perenar is an ESG and business management consultancy based in Santa Cruz do Sul, Brazil. They support companies of all sizes with services like carbon footprint inventories and offsetting, ESG strategy implementation, data protection (LGPD), risk and quality management (including ISO 9001), circular economy, reverse logistics, environmental licensing, and solid waste management.
Grupo Studio is Brazil’s largest corporate solutions hub, founded in 1998 in Porto Alegre, with a commercial headquarters in São Paulo. They offer over 40 integrated services—financial, tax, agribusiness, marketing, legal, accounting, business restructuring, and more—focused on boosting cash flow, performance, compliance, and Governance.
